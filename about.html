Our Objective

Rajasthan Handicrafts main aim is to keep alive sprite of our artisans and enlighten the beauty of Indian Handicraft and also to reach to those who are looking for crafts & decorative for their home as well as make Indian handicraft products available worldwide with highest choices available. Rajasthan Handicraft have only Eco friendly and Traditional Indian handicrafts at competitive prices.
Our philosophy is simple - produce fantastic, ethically sourced yet commercial products to maximize sales while maximizing benefits to both our artisans and customers.
We specialize in wooden handicrafts, marble handicrafts, marble painting, and other types of handicrafts in India. Most of the semi-antique elements have been given new life by the addition of modern inlay and overlay work by a dedicated team of skilled artisans, painters, and craftsmen.
